Our Founding & Vision

Founded in 2016 through the historic merger of several parties, Jubilee was born out of the belief that Kenya is stronger together.

We replaced division with unity and conflict with cooperation, creating a national political movement that transcends ethnic and regional lines.

Tusonge Mbele Pamoja — Let’s move forward together.

A Legacy of Transformation

Under the leadership of President Uhuru Kenyatta, the Jubilee Party has championed:

  • ✅ Massive infrastructure development – modern roads, railways, and bridges
  • ✅ Expanded access to electricity and clean energy
  • ✅ Investments in healthcare and education for all Kenyans
  • ✅ Economic growth that positions Kenya as Africa’s next powerhouse

In the 2013 and 2017 general elections, millions of Kenyans entrusted Jubilee with the mandate to build a new Kenya — and we delivered.
