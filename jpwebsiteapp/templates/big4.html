Transforming Kenya's Future

The Big Four Agenda represents Jubilee's commitment to accelerate Kenya's transformation into a newly industrializing, middle-income country providing a high quality of life to all its citizens by 2030.

These four priority areas were identified as fundamental to uplifting the lives of all Kenyans and ensuring the country's prosperity for generations to come.

Universal Healthcare

Achieving universal health coverage for all Kenyans

The government is committed to ensuring all Kenyans have access to affordable and quality healthcare through:

  • Expanding NHIF coverage to all households
  • Building new hospitals and upgrading existing facilities
  • Increasing healthcare workforce
  • Improving access to essential medicines

Affordable Housing

Providing decent and affordable housing for all Kenyans

The affordable housing program aims to deliver 500,000 affordable homes through:

  • Public-private partnerships
  • Innovative financing models
  • Modern construction technologies
  • Development of supporting infrastructure

Manufacturing

Growing the manufacturing sector to 15% of GDP

Key initiatives to boost manufacturing include:

  • Special Economic Zones
  • Industrial parks development
  • Support for SMEs and startups
  • Skills development programs
  • Improved ease of doing business

Food Security

Ensuring all Kenyans have access to affordable, nutritious food

The food security pillar focuses on:

  • Increasing agricultural productivity
  • Improving food storage and distribution
  • Promoting irrigation projects
  • Supporting smallholder farmers
  • Reducing post-harvest losses

Who are Involved in Implementing the Big 4 Agenda?

Achieving these ambitious goals requires collaboration from all sectors of society

You, the Kenyan Citizen

Every Kenyan has a role to play - from adopting healthy lifestyles to participating in housing schemes and supporting local manufacturing.

The National Government

Providing policy framework, funding, and coordination of national programs across all four pillars.

County Governments

Implementing devolved functions like healthcare services, local housing projects, and agricultural extension services.

Local & Foreign Investors

Bringing capital, expertise and innovation to drive large-scale projects in housing, manufacturing and agribusiness.

Development Partners

International organizations and foreign governments providing technical and financial support for development programs.
